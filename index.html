How to train your dog

First try to decipher what type of dog you have. It might be hard at first but just take a look at your pup.

If you can't figure it out, then google your dogs attributes and see what happens.

Dog breed is important

Try using this website. This will allow you to figure out the dog breed which will in turn allow you to train them better. Knowing the conditions and criteria for each type of dog is often overlooked and not used enough. This is especially good for dog owners who adopted their dogs and refer to them as "mutts"
